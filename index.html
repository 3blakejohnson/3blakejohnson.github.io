My Personal Interests

    One of my all-time favorite things to do is to ski. There's nothing better than zooming down a mountain with the cold, crisp air whooshing across your face and past your ears. I started skiing when I was probably around 9 or 10 years old, and have loved it ever since. Whenever I can find a deal on skiing, I jump on it and seize the opportunity.

    Another one of my hobbies is Golf. I started golfing when I was around 10 years old, and really only went because I wanted to drive the golf cart. Overtime, however, I realized that it was actually fun. I started out always going with my dad, and later started going with my friends. A couple of my friends were on the High School golf team, so I always felt like I wasn't a very good golfer when I went with them, because I would always score a lot higher than them.

    Lastly, I also have always had a love for technology. It started out with my fascination for computers and video games when I was young, and continued to grow as more technological advances came about. In High School, I took lots of programming classes, and realized it was very interesting to me. That's when I decided to study Computer Science in College, and now here I am!
